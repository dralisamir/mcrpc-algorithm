Introduction

PSA kinetics (PSADT/velocity): In mCRPC, PSA kinetics are not used to select therapy; avoid PSA-only progression calls without clinical or imaging evidence.

Biomarkers

HRR genes & BRCA

HRR genes include BRCA1/2, PALB2, ATM, CHEK2, CDK12. BRCA1/2 alterations predict strong benefit from PARPi strategies.

MSI-H/dMMR

MSI-H/dMMR predicts response to pembrolizumab; rare in prostate cancer but clinically relevant.

Monitoring Strategy

  • Labs: PSA, testosterone, CBC, CMP.
  • Imaging: CT CAP + bone scan every 3–6 months or earlier if clinically indicated.
  • PSMA PET: Only for Lu-PSMA eligibility; not routine for response assessment.
