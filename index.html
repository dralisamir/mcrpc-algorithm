Introduction

This clinical handbook summarizes the 2025 ASCO guideline update for metastatic castration-resistant prostate cancer (mCRPC), integrating:

  • Therapeutic selection based on prior castration-sensitive phase (CSPC) exposure
  • HRR and MSI/MMR biomarkers
